CONCEPT of the PROJECT

Background of the business


Inspired by the convenience and flexibility of the Uber/Grab ride-hailing service, we came up with the idea of creating a spa service that worked in a similar way.


With the help of technology and a network of skilled and experienced spa professionals, we launched the platform, allowing customers to book a spa treatment on-demand.

CONCEPT of the PROJECT

Background of the business


Today, the platform is known as the go-to destination for people who want to relax, rejuvenate, and feel their best, without the hassle of traditional spa bookings.


With this innovative approach and commitment to customer satisfaction, SPAce aims to transform the way people think about spa services.

CONCEPT of the PROJECT

What We Do


Bridge the gap between the clients and the specialists via this online gateway to connect them.


The clients to be pampered And The specialists to provide their services

CONCEPT of the PROJECT

How It Works


  • Users would access the website through their web browser.

  • Users would create an account and input their payment information.

  • Users would then be able to browse a variety of spa services, including massages, facials, and other treatments.

  • Users would select the service they want, and the app would display the available service providers in their area.

  • Users would select their preferred service provider based on factors such as their ratings and availability.

CONCEPT of the PROJECT

How It Works


  • Users would then be able to book their appointment, including the date, time, and location where they would like the service to be performed.

  • The service provider would then receive the booking request and confirm the appointment.

  • The service provider would arrive at the specified location to provide the spa service.

  • After the service is completed, the user would pay through the app, and they would have the option to rate and provide feedback on the service provider.

Overall, a spa website that works like Uber or Grab would provide users with a convenient and flexible way to access spa services on-demand, without the need for advanced booking or scheduling. This would make it easier for people to relax, de-stress, and take care of their bodies in a way that fits their busy schedules.
